MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

  1. Link to other I.T professionals both in Ghana and abroad
  2. Get to know and share ideas with other I.T professionals locally and internationally
  3. Acquire mores skills and upgrade yourself in the field of I.T for free
  4. Through interdependency programme, members would be offered free training in server, database, programming, web developing, hardware, application software, graphic designing, network, etc.
  5. Committed members upon grant received, would be given free laptops for GNAIT works, research making, and innovative works.
  6. Members who would like to remain in the teaching field would be given the required teaching and learning materials such as laptops, I.C.T textbooks, and projector for effective teaching of I.C.T subject nationwide
  7. Members would be assisted to gain employment in both public and private sectors
  8. GNAIT Members teaching in private schools would be given the necessary TLMs, offer their schools free ICT textbooks and computers and through this GNAIT Executive would meet the schools' authorities to negotiate a good salary.
  9. Schools would be allowed to use GNAIT Resource and Research Centers nationwide.
  10. GNAIT would repair computers in a member school for free and provide the right educational programs
  11. GNAIT would sponsor some members to upgrade themselves abroad to support GNAIT
  12. GNAIT would arrange to let selected members go in for two weeks or a month training on our innovative ideologies abroad.
  13. Committed members would have the opportunity to partake in the launching of GNAIT in United Kingdom, Denmark, Germany and United State
  14. Selected members will go abroad for fundraising on yearly basis
  15. Committed members will be employed by GNAIT to work in our I.T Firms and fashion designing centers nationwide either part-time or full time.
  16. GNAIT will employ committed members to take care of charity shops we will open in United Kingdom, Denmark, Germany, and United States.
  17. Allowances will be given to all committed members upon grants received either monthly or yearly
  18. GNAIT members will be given opportunities in school exchange programmes (teacher exchange programme)
  19. Team of sponsors and donors from abroad will meet GNAIT members to party and tour Ghana every summer.
  20. Team of GNAIT members will join sponsors, donors and partners abroad during summer
  21. Financial support will be given to ICT teachers to form and run ICT Clubs their schools
