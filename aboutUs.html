ABOUT US

Ghana National Association of ICT Teachers is an association uniting ICT instructors from both public and private schools and I.T professionals in Ghana in the cause of promoting ICT education. GNAIT help to train young men and women in the field of IT offer teaching and learning materials and establish IT centers for educational purposes intiated in 2016 by teachers from united Kingdom ,German,y Denmark and ICT and IT professionals from Ghana.GNAIT

GNAIT has quickly dominated the entire nation and we have members in all the ten regions of Ghana. We aim at supporting deprive schools by establishing computer laboratories for them, organising workshops for students and ICT teachers and organising ICT quizzes for students nationwide. We are passionate to train unskilled people to acquire future career in I.T and support I.T professionals in diverse ways; establish businesses, expand businesses, guide and promote their goods and services. It is our priority to seek welfare of I.T professionals, ICT teachers and students in Ghana. .

Mission

1. Our mission is to mobilise all ICT teachers, IT professionals and Academicians in Ghana to link up with the same caliber of people internationally to help us come out and implement our IT ideologies to develop our dear nation, Ghana.

2. Our vision it to transform Ghanaian traditional methodologies where chalk and chalkboards are use in teaching and learning into digital learning where projectors and smartboards are used to improve the academic standard in Ghanaian education.
