OUR AIMS

    1. To promote academic performance of students in Ghana through I. T
    2. To transform traditional methodologies into digital teaching and learning (using electronic whiteboards in classrooms)
    3. G.N.A.I.T will put up strategies that will support and compel GES, Ministry of Education and other relevant bodies to make ICT subject examinable at the second cycle institutions in Ghana.
    4. To deepen or increase the interest of students to like I.C.T subject and used ICT in the right context
    5. To have a common ICT Textbook approved by Ghana Education Service for Basic Schools and Senior High Schools in Ghana.
    6. To equip ICT Teachers to improve teaching and learning of ICT subject in Ghana
    7. To improve the teaching of ICT in Ghana, and to acquaint member of new trends in ICT and ICT syllabuses.
    8. To initiate and develop links between teachers of ICT, their pupils and other institutions requiring the application of ICT
    9. To offer guidance and counselling to students and parents for the selection of courses in I.T per job market
    10. To Support the education of the brilliant but deserving students
    11. To train unskilled young men and women in the field of I.T for them to acquire future career
    12. To establish computer training centers and educational research centers nationwide
    13. For Ghana Education Service to permit GNAIT to recruit ICT teachers into the public sector
    14. To build a secured information system for Ghana Education Service nationwide and set up a system to facilitate their day to day activities.
    15. To have a common educational website full of resources for both teachers and students
    16. To post ICT Teachers to private schools in Ghana and I.T professionals to private I.T companies in Ghana and abroad
    17. To support I.T professionals in GNAIT to expand their businesses
    18. To introduce charity shops in Ghana and to have charity shops abroad for ICT equipment to support Ghana Education Service.
    19. To transform the transportation ticketing system into electronics where we will use oyster card in Ghana
    20. To mobilise I.T professionals and I.C.T teachers in Ghana to team up to promote ICT Education and innovate new things to develop the country
    21. To link with international I.T professionals, I.T companies, and I.T experts to help complete and implement innovative ideologies for national development
    22. To supply energy to some parts of Ghana for national development
    23. To bring in IT companies to establish in Ghana to create jobs for IT people
    24. Help to form ICT Clubs in Schools in Ghana
    25. Train headmasters in I.T to improve school development
    26. Innovate technological means to substitute the use of log book, attendance book, etc
