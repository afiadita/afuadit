ABOUT US

OUR AIMS

    • To promote academic performance of students in Ghana through I. T
    • To transform traditional methodologies into digital teaching and learning (using electronic whiteboards in classrooms)
    • G.N.A.I.T will put up strategies that will support and compel GES, Ministry of Education and other relevant bodies to make ICT subject examinable at the second cycle institutions in Ghana.
    • To deepen or increase the interest of students to like I.C.T subject and used ICT in the right context
    • To have a common ICT Textbook approved by Ghana Education Service for Basic Schools and Senior High Schools in Ghana.
    • To equip ICT Teachers to improve teaching and learning of ICT subject in Ghana
    • To improve the teaching of ICT in Ghana, and to acquaint member of new trends in ICT and ICT syllabuses.
    • To initiate and develop links between teachers of ICT, their pupils and other institutions requiring the application of ICT
    • To offer guidance and counselling to students and parents for the selection of courses in I.T per job market
    • To Support the education of the brilliant but deserving students
    • To train unskilled young men and women in the field of I.T for them to acquire future career
    • To establish computer training centers and educational research centers nationwide
    • For Ghana Education Service to permit GNAIT to recruit ICT teachers into the public sector
    • To build a secured information system for Ghana Education Service nationwide and set up a system to facilitate their day to day activities.
    • To have a common educational website full of resources for both teachers and students
    • To post ICT Teachers to private schools in Ghana and I.T professionals to private I.T companies in Ghana and abroad
    • To support I.T professionals in GNAIT to expand their businesses
    • To introduce charity shops in Ghana and to have charity shops abroad for ICT equipment to support Ghana Education Service.
    • To transform the transportation ticketing system into electronics where we will use oyster card in Ghana
    • To mobilise I.T professionals and I.C.T teachers in Ghana to team up to promote ICT Education and innovate new things to develop the country
    • To link with international I.T professionals, I.T companies, and I.T experts to help complete and implement innovative ideologies for national development
    • To supply energy to some parts of Ghana for national development
    • To bring in IT companies to establish in Ghana to create jobs for IT people
    • Help to form ICT Clubs in Schools in Ghana
    • Train headmasters in I.T to improve school development
    • Innovate technological means to substitute the use of log book, attendance book, etc
