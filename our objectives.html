OUR OBJECTIVES

OUR OBJECTIVES

  1. To offer schools in Ghana ICT textbooks to enhance teaching and learning of ICT
    2. subject in both basic and high schools
  2. To offer schools in Ghana IT equipment such as computers, printers, webcams,
    3. projectors, modems, pen drives, scanners, photocopiers, etc. to facilitate teaching and learning
  3. To offer and install electronic (interactive) boards in schools (deprived) for effective teaching and learning.
  4. To offer training and guidance to ICT teachers/instructors in Ghanaian schools through workshops by our ICT team to shape them to teach in accordance to national curriculum/ICT syllabus
  5. To establish computer schools in our communities to train young people to acquire skills to work with
  6. To raise awareness of Information Technology and increased understanding of the use I.T
  7. To promote Information Technology in deprived areas of Ghana
  8. To enhance pupils and students experiences through active learning of ICT
  9. To improve communication skills among pupils and students
  10. To train staff in global citizenship and critical thinking in IT field
  11. To explore the benefits of active learning of ICT
  12. To publicise and promote IT projects in the rural areas/communities
  13. Be given opportunities to investigate or make research by the use of IT to solve problems
  14. Develop communication skills to be able to communicate with others worldwide.
  15. Acquire skills in the use of IT for creativity
  16. Acquire skills to work as a future career.
