WHO WE ARE

The Ghana National Association of ICT Teachers (GNAIT) is a registered Non-governmental organization consisting of ICT Teachers and IT Professionals from both public and private schools and Companies. We aim at promoting ICT education in Ghana, establish computer laboratories for deprive schools, implement IT ideologies to develop Ghana, establish computer schools for the deprived communities, and support the needy but brilliant students in Ghana. Technology has made the world a global village and countries that are well advanced in technology are leading the world in all areas. Ghana is a developing country and we believe that through technology it can be a developed one. Due to this fact, we, the ICT Teachers and IT intellectuals in Ghana have come together to put up strategies, programs, projects and innovative ideologies to develop our dear nation, Ghana.

OUR FOCUS

Our focus is to enrich Ghanaian education to empower students to become innovators and entrepreneurs’, help improve quality health services, implement strategies to supply energy to Ghanaian people to solve the low schedule challenges to create enabling environment for international investors, prevent people from sleeping in darkness in Ghana and eliminate unemployment among companies. Also our focus will be on mobilizing and training young people; orphans, school dropouts, unskilled young men and women who have no future career in the field of IT and fashion designing to add value to the life of the youth. It is our priority to assist students who are really brilliant but one way or the other needy in education.

We call on IT firms and IT experts around the world to help us complete and implement our innovative ideologies to develop Ghana as a country to rock shoulders with the developed countries. We have vast innovative ideas which could be implemented to move the country forward.
