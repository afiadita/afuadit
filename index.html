WHO WE ARE

We aim at promoting ICT education in Ghana, establish computer laboratories for deprive schools, implement IT ideologies to develop Ghana, establish computer schools for the deprived communities, and support the needy but brilliant students in Ghana.

OUR FOCUS

Our focus is to enrich Ghanaian education to empower students to become innovators and entrepreneurs, that is mobilizing and training young people; orphans, school dropouts, unskilled young men and women who have no future career in the field of IT and fashion designing to add value to the life of the youth. It is our priority to assist students who are really brilliant but one way or the other needy in education.

SUPPORT US

We call on IT firms and IT experts around the world to help us complete and implement our innovative ideologies to develop Ghana as a country to rock shoulders with the developed countries. We have vast innovative ideas which could be implemented to move the country forward.

ABOUT US

Ghana National Association of ICT Teachers is an association uniting ICT instructors from both public and private schools and I.T professionals in Ghana in the cause of promoting ICT education. GNAIT help to train young men and women in the field of IT offer teaching and learning materials and establish IT centers for educational purposes intiated in 2016 by teachers from united Kingdom ,Germany, Denmark and ICT and IT professionals from Ghana.

GNAIT has quickly dominated the entire nation and we have members in all the ten regions of Ghana. We aim at supporting deprive schools by establishing computer laboratories for them, organising workshops for students and ICT teachers and organising ICT quizzes for students nationwide.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to mobilise all ICT teachers, IT professionals and Academicians in Ghana to link up with the same caliber of people internationally to help us come out and implement our IT ideologies to develop our dear nation, Ghana.

OUR VISION

Our vision it to transform Ghanaian traditional methodologies where chalk and chalkboards are use in teaching and learning into digital learning where projectors and smartboards are used to improve the academic standard in Ghanaian education..

OUR PASSION

We are passionate to train unskilled people to acquire future career in I.T and support I.T professionals in diverse ways; establish businesses, expand businesses, guide and promote their goods and services. It is our priority to seek welfare of I.T professionals, ICT teachers and students in Ghana.

OUR GOALS

  1. Every pupil / student shall have access to computer by 2030
  2. Every pupil / student shall acquire computer skills by 2030
  3. 40% of Ghanaian schools shall have access to electronic whiteboards and projectors installed in classrooms by 2030
  4. By 2030 Ghana Education shall transform from chalk and chalkboard teaching to digital teaching and learning
  5. ICT subject shall be examinable at the second cycle institutions in Ghana by 2021 theoretically
  6. Both Practical and Theory ICT examinations shall be conducted in Ghana by 2030.
  7. National ICT quiz shall be implemented to promote ICT education by 2018
  8. ICT clubs shall be formed in all the schools in Ghana by 2020
  9. ICT teachers will be well equipped by 2020
  10. Electronic ticketing system shall be implemented in Ghana 2025
  11. Um-employed graduates shall be offered training in I.T to gain employment or may be supported to establish their own business.
  12. Improve school management system in Ghana
